deejay.de goodies v0.1.4

Optimize your music discovery process with simple keyboard shortcuts and a pitch (playback rate) fader.

For the shortcuts to work make sure the search box isn't focused, by clicking somewhere outside of it or pressing Tab.


Shortcuts:
  • Space play/pause
  • Right/left arrow go forward/back 5 seconds
  • Down/up arrow play next/previous track
  • Page down/up play the next/previous vinyl in your list, cart or charts
  • +/- adjust the pitch (for now it's actually only playback rate)

Additionally, this extension allows you to see all the records you ordered by going to "Shipped Orders" in your account and clicking on "Show all records"
